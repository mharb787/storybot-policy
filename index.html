Owner-operated content automation

Story Video Bot

Story Video Bot creates short vertical Arabic storytelling videos and posts them to TikTok accounts that explicitly authorize the app.

What the app does

The app helps its owner create 9:16 short-form story videos. It fetches public story sources, rewrites them into original Arabic scripts, generates narration and subtitles, renders an MP4 video, and sends the finished video to Telegram for visibility.

Login Kit TikTok Login Kit is used only so the TikTok account owner can authorize the app.
Content Posting API The app uses TikTok's Content Posting API to upload or direct-post generated videos to the authorized account.
AI-generated content Generated videos are marked as AI-generated content through the posting API when published.

Authorized account workflow

The owner controls video generation from a Telegram bot. After the owner connects TikTok, the bot can upload videos as drafts or direct-post them to the authorized account, depending on the permissions approved by TikTok.
