Owner-operated content automation

Story Video Bot

Story Video Bot creates short vertical Arabic storytelling videos and uploads them only to YouTube channels that explicitly authorize the app.

What the app does

The app helps its owner create 9:16 short-form story videos. It fetches public story sources, rewrites them into original Arabic scripts, generates narration, optional illustrated scenes, and subtitles, renders an MP4 video, and sends the finished video to Telegram for review.

Google OAuth Google OAuth is used only so the YouTube channel owner can authorize video uploads.
YouTube Data API The app uses the YouTube Data API to upload generated videos to the authorized channel.
AI-generated content Generated videos are marked as containing synthetic media when uploaded.

Authorized account workflow

The owner controls video generation from a Telegram bot. After the owner connects YouTube, the bot can upload videos to the authorized channel using the configured privacy setting.
