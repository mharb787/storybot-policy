Story Video Bot

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Story Video Bot is an automation tool used by its owner to generate short story videos and upload them to authorized accounts.

Information We Process

The service may process public story text, generated scripts, generated media files, Telegram chat identifiers configured by the owner, and OAuth tokens granted by the owner for social media publishing.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to fetch story candidates, generate videos, send videos to Telegram, and upload videos to connected social media accounts authorized by the owner.

Storage

The service may store generated videos, used story records, configuration values, and authorization tokens on the owner's server. Access is limited to the server owner and the automation process.

Sharing

The service shares generated media with third-party platforms only when configured and authorized by the account owner. It does not sell personal data.

Data Removal

The owner may remove generated media, stored records, and authorization tokens from the server at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the owner of the TikTok Developer application.
